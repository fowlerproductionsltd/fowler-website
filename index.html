About Us

Fowler Productions Ltd is a dynamic Ugandan company focused on creating practical, impactful solutions across diverse sectors. Our work spans printing, stationery, agricultural produce, school books, real estate, and transport. We aim to serve communities, schools, and businesses with reliability and purpose-driven innovation.

Our Services

  • Printing & Stationery: High-quality print materials for schools and businesses.
  • Agriculture: Supplying fresh produce and large-scale maize farming on 7 acres.
  • School Book Supply: Customized books delivered at the start of each term.
  • Real Estate: Affordable rental units on ground and first floor.
  • Transport Services: Reliable car transport using our Toyota Premio.
